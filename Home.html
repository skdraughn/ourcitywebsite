What Does Our App Offer?

Promotions

Posting promotions on  OUR CITY is a very simple process. Each promotional credit that a user purchases allows for one promotional post. Credits can be purchased below and have a price of $249.99 each or a subscription fee of $999.99 per month for unlimited promotional posts. One of the benefits of holding credits is that they act as a profile-verification tool; each account that is currently holding credits will have a golden verification badge adjacent to their username on the Promotions page of  OUR CITY.
